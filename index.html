Akan Names

The Day Names in the Akan People of Ghana and Ivory Coast

The Ghanian Culture is a rich Culture of Western Africa. This culture reflects in how they name their children.For every child born in Ghana, they are all given a first name which is usually referred to as their "day" name. These day names are also referred to as their "Akan" name and have further meanings concerning the soul and character of the person. These days names are influenced by two things: Your Gender and the Day of the week in whch you were born

If you were born as Male; your day name would be one of the below options depending on the day of the week you were born

  • Sunday : Kwasi
  • Monday : Kwadwo
  • Tuesday : Kwabena
  • Wednesday : Kwaku
  • Thursday : Yaw
  • Friday : Kofi
  • Saturday : Kwame

Alternately, if you were born as Female; your day name would be one of these options depending on the day of the week you were born

  • Sunday : Akosua
  • Monday : Adwoa
  • Tuesday : Abenaa
  • Wednesday : Akua
  • Thursday : Yaa
  • Friday : Afua
  • Saturday : Ama
